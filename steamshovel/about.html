about.

steamshovel. is a collaborative design accelerator that often closely resembles a venture studio. we combine resources from across our collective consulting network of experts in areas such as investing, entrepreneurship, design, product, engineering & specific industry verticals to create bespoke client service teams as well as to launch our own proprietary ventures.

with decades of combined experience & a reputation for excellence serving clients from the Fortune 20 to pre-seed startups, the team comprising our network possesses a wide variety of skills to help find, create & grow new sources of value. contact us in order to learn more about the skills, experience & capabilities we can bring to bear towards solving your organic growth challenges.
